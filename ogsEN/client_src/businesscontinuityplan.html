FAQ

Why do you need a BCP and DRP?

You need a business continuity and disaster recovery plan for operational purposes. These plans are often required by vendors, clients and financial institutions to confirm business sustainability.

How much time will it take to develop a BCP?

Timeframe of the projects depends on its complexity. On average, it takes between 2 and 3 weeks.

Who will be engaged on my project?

Our team currently includes 15 risk-advisory consultants. Depending on the industry in which your company operates, we will create a project team consisting of 3-4 experts with relevant expertise and 5 years of experience in developing of BCPs. In addition, we will reach out to experts with specific expertise if needed.

What are the payment terms?

Our standard terms suppose 50% prepayment and the remaining 50% when the project is complete and you are fully satisfied.
